IntraSurge Presents at ESA 2025 Annual Meeting

  • May 2, 2025

The Annual Meeting of the European Surgical Association (ESA) is a leading forum for advancing clinical and experimental surgery, surgical education, and the socioeconomic dimensions of surgical care.

We were proud to have Romy van Jaarsveld, representing the IntraSurge team, present our latest research: “Surgical Guidance in Robot-Assisted Minimally Invasive Esophagectomy (RAMIE) using Deep Learning: A Retrospective Cohort Study.” The presentation was followed by a lively and insightful discussion with colleagues from across Europe. We’re grateful for the opportunity to contribute to ESA’s mission of promoting surgical excellence and innovation, and we look forward to ongoing collaboration with experts in the field.
